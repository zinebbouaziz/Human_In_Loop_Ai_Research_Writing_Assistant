# CoWriteX – Human-in-the-Loop AI Research Writing Assistant

CoWriteX is a human-in-the-loop AI platform designed to support academic writing by combining AI assistance with continuous user control and validation.  
The system helps researchers write, refine, organize and reference scientific papers while preserving methodological rigor, traceability and transparency.

---

## 🚀 Project Vision

Writing high-quality scientific papers is time-consuming and requires:

- structured organization,
- reliable referencing,
- consistent academic tone,
- and full control over AI-generated content.

CoWriteX addresses these challenges by introducing a **human-in-the-loop workflow** where every AI suggestion must be reviewed and accepted or rejected by the user.

---

## ✨ Core Principles

- Human control over all AI outputs
- Transparent and traceable AI suggestions
- Structured research writing workflow
- Context-aware assistance across the whole paper
- Integrated literature and citation workflow

---

## 🧩 Main Features

### 1. Paper & Methodology Management
- Create and manage multiple research papers
- Start from predefined methodologies (IMRAD, thesis structure, etc.)
- Fully customize and reorder sections

### 2. Rich Writing Environment
- Section-based rich text editor
- Automatic saving
- Clean academic writing interface

### 3. Human-in-the-Loop AI Assistance
- Chat-based AI assistant for writing help
- AI suggestions and refinements are shown separately
- Accept or reject each AI suggestion
- All actions are logged

### 4. Context-Aware AI
- The AI is aware of:
  - the current section
  - the complete paper structure and content

### 5. Reference Management & Search
- Manual reference management
- Intelligent reference search with filters
- Accept or reject references before adding them

### 6. Citation & Literature Support
- Citation suggestions for selected paragraphs
- Insert citations at cursor position

### 7. Data Visualization
- Generate tables and figures from datasets
- Integrate visual results directly into the paper

### 8. Versioning
- Track paper versions
- Restore previous versions

### 9. Export
- Export paper to LaTeX
- Export paper to PDF

### 10. User Personalization
- Writing style preferences
- Research interests
- AI behavior preferences
